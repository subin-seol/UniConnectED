## Persona: Sophia

#### Basic Information
- **Full Name:** Sophia Kim
- **Role/Occupation:** Master in Business Analytics
- **Age Group:** 18-25
- **Location:** Australia (Originally from Korea)
- **Tech Proficiency:** Medium

#### Background
Sophia is an international student in her first year of the Master of Business Analytics program at the University of Melbourne. She completed her undergraduate degree in Korea and came to Australia hoping to gain some international work experience. Whilst Sophia is confident with academic work, she finds the career systems in Australia to be unfamiliar and intimidating. She’s currently looking for part-time work and internships but doesn’t have a strong professional network here.

#### Goals and Motivations
- To find part-time work or internships during her study
- To build a local network of professionals and peers

#### Pain Points and Challenges
- Limited familiarity with Australian job applications, etiquette, and resumes
- Feels disconnected due to language barriers and cultural differences
- Unsure how to start career-related conversations without getting referrals

#### Behaviour and Needs
- **Preferred Communication Channels:** Email, LinkedIn
- **Information Sources:** University career services, workshops, student associations
- **Common Tools & Technologies Used:** Excel, Power BI, Google Workspace

#### Interaction with the System
Sophia uses UniConnectED primarily to explore part-time work and internship opportunities that are open to international students. Since she's unfamiliar with the Australian job market, she looks for clear onboarding and prompts that guide her through profile completion. She values being able to showcase her degree, language skills, and work eligibility.

#### User Stories (For This Persona)
> "As an international student, I want to find job and internship opportunities open to international applicants, so that I can gain experience while studying."

> "As an international student, I want to connect with mentors who understand my background, so that I can navigate the Australian job market with more confidence."

## Persona : Alex 

#### Basic Information
- **Full Name:** Alex Morgan
- **Role/Occupation:** Talent Acquisition Manager
- **Age Group:** 30-45
- **Location:** Sydney, Australia
- **Tech Proficiency:** Medium

#### Background
Alex has over 10 years of experience in human resources and recruitment, working mainly at mid-sized companies. His recent focus has been campus recruitment and building partnerships with universities to bring in fresh talent. Whilst Alex previously used platforms like LinkedIn and SEEK to search for job candidates, he is now actively exploring other solutions for hiring university students and recent graduates.

#### Goals and Motivations
- Build a reliable pipeline of university talent for internships and graduate programs.
- Reduce time taken to access potential, motivated student candidates.
- Expand the company visibility among students to attract a larger pool of candidates.

#### Pain Points and Challenges
- Difficulty in identifying candidates who are both academically strong and culturally aligned with the company.
- Competition with larger companies that dominate the more traditional job platforms.
- Limited visibility into student skills beyond academic transcripts, making overall candidate evaluation more difficult.

#### Behaviour and Needs
- **Preferred Communication Channels:** Email, LinkedIn, UniConnected’s messaging system.
- **Information Sources:** Industry reports, HR blogs, university career services.
- **Common Tools & Technologies Used:** Microsoft Office, Zoom, LinkedIn.

#### Interaction with the System
Alex uses UniConnected to directly browse student profiles filtered by skills, majors, and interests. He would like to have a recommendation feature to quickly shortlist candidates in the platform. Also, Alex would like to directly message students through the platform and communicate with shortlisted candidates.

#### User Stories
> "As Alex, I want to quickly identify students with the right technical and soft skills, so that I can reduce hiring time and secure talent for our graduate program."

> "As Alex, I want to collect the suitable candidates, so that I can reach out to them for future opportunities even if they aren’t chosen for this time."

## Persona: Helen

#### Basic Information
- **Full Name:** Helen Carter
- **Role/Occupation:** Director of a Community Welfare Organisation
- **Age Group:** 45-60
- **Location:** Melbourne, Australia
- **Tech Proficiency:** Low

#### Background
Helen has been leading a community welfare organisation for over 20 years. Her work involves coordinating programs that support vulnerable groups such as elderly care, youth support, and other community development projects. She has a background in social work and management, but has limited exposure to advanced digital platforms since her career has mostly been focused on building relationships and ensuring social impact, rather than dealing with technology.

#### Goals and Motivations
- Recruit university student as volunteers or interns to support community programs.
- Reduce time to access potential, motivated student candidates.
- Use a simple and accessible platform that doesn’t require high-level technical knowledge.

#### Pain Points and Challenges
- Finds many digital recruitment platforms and job boards to be too complex for non-technical users.
- Believes that resumes do not allow her to get to know the candidate well.
- Has limited time and staff to review candidates extensively, so needs tools that simplify the process.

#### Behaviour and Needs
- **Preferred Communication Channels:** Email, Phone calls, Phone message.
- **Information Sources:** Word-of-mouth from other community partners， 
- **Common Tools & Technologies Used:** Microsoft Word and Excel

#### Interaction with the System
Helen uses UniConnected primarily to look for students who want to join her program as interns or volunteers. She prioritises features that allow her to get to know the candidate beyond professional skills. She expects the interface to be clean and simple, with filters that highlight soft skills and community service experience, rather than just grades and resume.

#### User Stories
> "As Helen, I want the platform to be easy to use, so that I can look for some suitable candidates without spending too much time on learning."

#### Additional Notes   
Helen is more likely to use UniConnected if it helps her quickly identify trustworthy students who can make a positive difference, without having to spend too much time learning or navigating a complex system
