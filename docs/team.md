## Team Communication Plan

### Objective
Ensure **productive standups**, clear **visibility of progress**, and **timely blocker/dependency management**.

---

### Meeting Structure

| Meeting             | When                        | Duration   | Platform | Notes                                                                 |
|---------------------|-----------------------------|------------|----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Standups**        | Mondays & Thursdays, 8:00PM | 30–60 mins | Zoom     | Task board walk-through, progress, blockers, and dependencies |
| **Peer Programming**| Wednesdays, 8:00PM          | Optional   | Discord  | Collaborative coding and troubleshooting session                       |

---

### Expectations

#### Standups
- Run through the **task board** (led by Scrum Master).
- Each member updates:
  - **Progress** on tasks
  - **Blockers** (if any)
  - **Dependencies** (esp. Team Wombat)

#### Task Management
- Every task should have:
  - **Expected Date of Discovery (EDD):**
    - When you will understand the task and identify blockers.
  - **Expected Completion Date (ECD):**
    - When you expect to finish the task.
- Post EDD & ECD as **GitHub comments** and notify the team on Discord.

#### Blockers
- At the **start of each sprint/task**, take time to identify possible blockers.
- Raise blockers **early** (ideally by the EDD).

---

### Communication Norms

| Area            | Expectation                                                                 |
|-----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Discord Role**| Use`team` for team-wide updates and visibility |
| **Thread Updates** | First message format: <br> `Update [task/issue]: [progress note]` <br> `I need help from @person cc: @team` |
| **Platforms**   | - **Zoom:** Standups <br> - **Discord:** Peer programming and async updates   |
